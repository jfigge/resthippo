Why switch

Built for developers, not dashboards

Your requests stay private

Every request runs natively on your machine and is stored in local files. No cloud workspace means your tokens, internal URLs, and payloads never leave your laptop.

No sign-in wall

Download, open, send a request. There's no mandatory account, no "create a team" prompt, and no feature held hostage behind a login or a subscription.

Depth without plugins

OAuth 2.0 & OIDC (every grant type), AWS SigV4, NTLM, Digest, mTLS, GraphQL, WebSockets, SSE, captures, tests, and pre/post scripts — all in the box, all free.

Already using Postman or Insomnia? Switch in minutes.

Rest Hippo imports your existing collections directly — Postman, Insomnia, OpenAPI 3 / Swagger 2, HAR, and cURL. Bring your requests, environments, and variables across, then export back out anytime (redaction-aware) so you're never locked in. Need a single request? Paste a curl command and Rest Hippo builds it for you.

Try the API client that respects you

Free, open source, and fully offline. No account, no telemetry — just install it and send a request.
